Momo

Description

These traditional dumplings are filled with a choice of minced meat or vegetables, seasoned with aromatic spices, and encased in a delicate dough. 
    Steamed to perfection, Momos offer a delightful blend of flavors and textures, served with a zesty dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • 250g all-purpose flour
  • 200g ground meat (beef or pork) or finely chopped vegetables
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped cilantro (optional)
  • Oil for greasing

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour with water to form a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for about 30 minutes.
  2. For the filling, mix the ground meat or vegetables with chopped onions, garlic, ginger, salt, pepper, and cilantro (if using).
  3. Roll out the dough and cut it into circles. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each circle.
  4. Fold the edges of the circle together, sealing the filling inside to form dumplings.
  5. Steam the momos in a steamer for about 15-20 minutes until the dough is cooked and the filling is cooked through.
  6. Serve hot with a spicy dipping sauce.
