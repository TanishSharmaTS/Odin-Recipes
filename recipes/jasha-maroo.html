Jasha Maroo

Description

Jasha Maroo is a popular Bhutanese dish consisting of seasoned shredded chicken, though a vegetarian version swaps the chicken for mushrooms or tofu.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped (adjust according to spice preference)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add chopped onions. Sauté until they turn translucent.
  2. Add minced garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for a minute or until fragrant.
  3. Put the chicken pieces into the pan and cook until they are lightly browned.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and form a thick sauce.
  5. Sprinkle turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and salt over the chicken mixture. Mix well to coat the chicken evenly with the spices.
  6. Cover the pan and let the chicken simmer on low-medium heat until it's cooked through and tender, stirring occasionally. If needed, add a little water to prevent sticking and to achieve desired consistency.
  7. Once cooked, garnish with chopped cilantro if desired.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice or Bhutanese red rice.
